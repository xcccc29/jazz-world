# 🎷 Jazz World

> *My way of exploring the classical to jazz path*

Jazz World is a personal journey documenting the exploration of music theory, improvisation, and the transition from classical training into the world of jazz.

---

## 🎼 About the Project

Classical music gives you discipline, precision, and a deep understanding of harmony and structure. Jazz takes all of that and adds spontaneity, freedom, and conversation between musicians.

This project serves as a living notebook for that journey — covering concepts, exercises, repertoire, and reflections along the way.

---

## 🗺️ The Path: Classical → Jazz

| Stage | Classical Foundation | Jazz Application |
|-------|----------------------|-----------------|
| Scales | Major / Minor / Modes | Bebop, Blues, Pentatonic |
| Harmony | Functional harmony, voice leading | Extended chords (7ths, 9ths, 13ths), substitutions |
| Rhythm | Strict time, meter | Swing feel, syncopation, polyrhythm |
| Improvisation | Cadenzas, ornamentation | Free improvisation, chord-tone soloing |
| Ear Training | Interval recognition, dictation | Transcription, "playing by ear" |

---

## 📚 Topics Covered

- **Music Theory** – intervals, chord construction, Roman numeral analysis
- **Jazz Harmony** – ii-V-I progressions, tritone substitution, modal harmony
- **Scales & Modes** – Dorian, Mixolydian, Lydian dominant, altered scale
- **Rhythm & Feel** – swing eighth notes, comping patterns, syncopation
- **Transcription** – learning solos by ear from jazz masters
- **Repertoire** – jazz standards explored along the journey
